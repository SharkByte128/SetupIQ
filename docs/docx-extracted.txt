Setup Tips:

🔧⚙️Ball Differential Tuning Advice

Tighten the ball diff fully, then back off about 1/8 to 1/4 turn to allow smooth operation without slipping.

The diff should turn smoothly by hand without grit or binding.

Avoid overtightening, which can cause the diff to act like a locked diff and reduce smooth differential action.

If the diff slips or makes a barking sound under power, tighten it slightly.

Regularly check and lubricate the diff with silicone or diff grease for optimal performance and longevity.

Adjust tightness based on track conditions and personal driving feel.
